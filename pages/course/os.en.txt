Books
======
* `Operating System Concepts, by Silberschatz, Galvin and Gagne, 9/E(2012) <http://www.os-book.com>`_
* `Operating Systems: Internals and Design, 8/E(2011), by William Stallings <http://williamstallings.com/OperatingSystems/>`_
* `Modern Operating Systems, 4/E(2014), by  Andrew S. Tanenbaum and Herbert Bos <http://www.pearsonhighered.com/educator/product/Modern-Operating-Systems/9780133591620.page>`_
* `Schaum's outline of operating systems(2002), by J Archer Harris <http://www.goodreads.com/book/show/664165.Schaum_s_Outline_of_Operating_Systems>`_
* `Operating Systems: Three Easy Pieces(2018), by Remzi H. Arpaci-Dusseau and Andrea C. Arpaci-Dusseau <https://pages.cs.wisc.edu/~remzi/OSTEP/#book-chapters>`_
